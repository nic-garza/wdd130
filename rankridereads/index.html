Come And See What Bull Riding Is All About

A Long History

Bull Riding came onto the scence in America for the first time in 1852 at the Lone Star Fair held in Corpus Christi, Texas. The sport stems from a variant of Mexican bull fighting called 'Jaripeo' where riders would literally ride the bull to death. Thankfully that is so far from the sport that it is today. From our southern neighbors sport of Jaripeo, the great sport of American bull riding was born.

Today's Rodeo

Today's Natinal Finals Rodeo (NFR), organized by the PRCA, is the premier championship rodeo event and is held the first full week of December each year in Las Vegas. In addition to barrel racing, the rodeo consists of bareback riding, steer wrestling, team roping, saddle-bronc riding, tie-down roping, and bull riding.
